Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

TeamKitX collects the following information to provide our sports equipment management services:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and team affiliations
  • Team Data: Team names, member rosters, equipment records, and assignment information
  • Equipment Data: Item details, photos, condition records, and location tracking
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Photo Data: Equipment photos uploaded for documentation purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our equipment management services
  • Track equipment inventory, assignments, and returns
  • Enable team coordination and equipment distribution
  • Process damage charges and late return fees
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important service notifications and alerts
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • With your team members as part of normal app functionality
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (Firebase, cloud storage)
  • With TeamWalletX when integration is enabled (for automated penalty creation)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

All third-party services are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored on Google Firebase servers with enterprise-grade security
  • Equipment photos stored in secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication required for all operations
  • Automatic data backups for reliability

Photo Data and Privacy

Equipment photos are used solely for documentation and condition tracking:

  • Photos are visible only to your team members
  • No facial recognition or identification technology is used
  • Photos can be deleted at any time
  • Stored securely with restricted access
  • Not used for marketing or shared with third parties

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@teamkitx.com

TeamWalletX Integration

When you enable TeamWalletX integration:

  • Member data is synchronized between both apps
  • Damage and late return charges can create penalties in TeamWalletX
  • You can disconnect integration at any time
  • Both apps maintain separate privacy controls
  • Data sharing is limited to necessary information only

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Equipment history: Retained for accountability and audit purposes
  • Photos: Retained until manually deleted or account closure
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days of deletion request
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer for compliance

Children's Privacy

TeamKitX is designed for teams and may be used by minors under supervision:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Team administrators must ensure appropriate consents are obtained
  • Parents can request access to or deletion of their child's data
  • Enhanced privacy protections apply to minor users

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Updates posted on our website

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: info@teamkitx.app
Website: www.teamkitx.app/privacy-policy
