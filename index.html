What's the difference between Player, Staff, Coach, and Admin roles?

+

Our role-based system ensures appropriate access levels for different team members:

  • Player: Focus on personal performance with achievements, statistics, and team communication
  • Staff: Support team operations with analytics dashboards and event management capabilities
  • Coach: Lead the team with advanced management tools, penalty assignment, and strategic insights
  • Admin: Complete system control including financial management, user permissions, and comprehensive reporting

Each role builds upon the previous tier, ensuring seamless team hierarchy and appropriate feature access.

How does the penalty tracking system work?

+

Our automated penalty system streamlines fine management and ensures accountability:

  • Custom Categories: Create penalty types specific to your team's rules (late arrivals, missed training, etc.)
  • Automatic Calculations: Set fixed amounts or percentage-based fines with instant computation
  • Payment Tracking: Monitor who has paid, pending amounts, and payment history
  • Reminder System: Automated notifications for outstanding penalties
  • Financial Reports: Comprehensive analytics on team penalty trends and collections

Coaches can assign penalties instantly, while admins maintain complete financial oversight and control.
