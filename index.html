What is TeamKitX and how does it help sports teams?

+

TeamKitX is a comprehensive sports equipment management platform that revolutionizes how teams track inventory, manage assignments, and coordinate kit distribution.

Our app helps teams with:

  • Inventory Tracking: Complete visibility of all equipment, conditions, and locations
  • Assignment Management: Track who has what equipment and when it's due back
  • Member Management: Player size profiles and equipment history
  • Real-time Analytics: Inventory value, utilization rates, and condition monitoring
  • Photo Documentation: Visual records of equipment condition

How does the assignment tracking system work?

+

Our smart assignment system makes equipment checkout and return effortless:

  • Easy Assignment: Select item, choose member, set return date - done in seconds
  • Condition Tracking: Document equipment condition at handout and return with photos
  • Overdue Alerts: Automatic notifications for late returns
  • Charge Calculation: Automatically calculate damage and late fees
  • Complete History: Full audit trail of all assignments and returns

Everything is tracked in real-time with instant updates across all devices.
